# Creative Director

**UMBRAL**

**One-line Pitch:** A shadow seeks reunification with its lost owner by manipulating light sources in a world where darkness reveals truth and illumination creates paths.

**Core Mechanic:** You exist only in shadow—when light hits you, you fade and lose agency. You manipulate light sources (lanterns, fires, mirrors, windows) to cast new shadows, creating platforms, bridges, and hiding spots while solving environmental puzzles.

**Emotional Thesis:** Connection requires sacrifice. Sometimes you must diminish yourself to help others shine, and sometimes separation reveals what was always there.

**Three Pillar Mechanics:**

1. **Shadow Casting** - Rotate/move light sources to project your shadow onto walls, creating traversable paths and revealing hidden elements in the environment

2. **Light Vulnerability** - Direct light weakens you; you must time movements between light sources, using other objects' shadows as safe passage

3. **Memory Echoing** - Interact with shadow-memories of your owner at key locations, learning their story and gaining new shadow abilities (stretching, splitting, reforming)

**Progression Loop:** 
Navigate area → Manipulate light to create shadow paths → Discover owner's memory fragment → Gain new shadow ability → Access previously unreachable areas → Repeat until final reunification

**Visual Style:** 
Sumi-e ink painting aesthetic with dynamic brush strokes that respond to movement. Shadows are deep black calligraphy strokes. Light sources bloom with watercolor warmth. The world "paints itself" as you explore, with white paper gradually filled by your journey.

**Audio Style:** 
Minimal soundscape focused on light/shadow interactions—paper rustling, ink drops, brush strokes. Emotional moments punctuated by single shakuhachi flute notes. No music, only the rhythm of your shadow-steps and environmental audio poetry.

**Why This Has Never Been Made:** 
The technical challenge of real-time shadow manipulation as core gameplay (not just puzzle elements) combined with meaningful emotional narrative delivered entirely through environmental storytelling and shadow-memory interactions without any UI or text.

**6-Month Solo Dev Viability:** 
Unity with custom 2D lighting system. Procedural ink-brush visual effects. 4-5 compact levels with escalating light-puzzle complexity. Focus on perfecting the shadow-casting feel and one profound emotional arc rather than scope creep.
